our mission statement
to provide excellence THROUGH DECISIVE MILITARY CAPABILITY in all domains TO PROTECT NATIONAL SECURITY AND ENABLE STRATEGIC INDEPENDENCE aS A TRUSTED PART OF kerbol's DEFENCE COMMUNITY.

Founded in 2208, the Aontan Defence Consortium has grown from a pattern of industrial cooperation within Aontas and with select international partners into Kerbol's premiere integrated defence lobbies. Our heritage lies in the successes written by collaborations of the past.

Today, the ADC encompasses 40'000 employees across a dozen industrial partners in five independent nations and provides over 80% of Aontan defence hardware.

The Consortium is united by a single belief: that the kerbals who operate our platforms deserve equipment that meets any challenge.

Consortium Chair
I
Platform Integrity

Every platform we deliver undergoes a minimum 3-week acceptance trial programme. We do not place any systems into circulation that have not already been accepted into national use.

II
Sovereign Partnership

We believe allied nations have the right to understand, maintain, and evolve the systems they operate. Our contracts include local production and in-country support.

III
Open Improvement

Lessons from every operational deployment feed directly into our engineering cycle for future improvement. National partners have a right to modify contracted platforms.

Governance
CONSORTIUM
STRUCTURE

The Aontan Defence Consortium operates under a joint programme office with leadership drawn from the member companies. Each member retains full corporate independence and intellectual property rights over their respective technologies. Work-share arrangements are defined on a programme-by-programme basis in accordance with each member's core competencies and industrial capacity commitments.
